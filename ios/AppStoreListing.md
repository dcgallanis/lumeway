## Description
Life just changed. Now what?

Lumeway is your personal guide through life's hardest transitions. Whether you're navigating job loss, settling an estate, going through a divorce, filing for disability, planning a move, approaching retirement, or supporting a loved one through addiction recovery, Lumeway gives you a clear, step-by-step path forward.

No more guessing what to do next. No more missing deadlines. No more feeling overwhelmed.

HOW LUMEWAY WORKS

Choose your transition, and Lumeway builds a personalized action plan just for you. Each checklist item tells you exactly what to do, why it matters, and how to do it, in plain language anyone can follow.

YOUR PERSONALIZED CHECKLIST
Every transition comes with a phased checklist: First 24 Hours, First Week, First Month, and beyond. Check off tasks as you go, skip what does not apply, and always know your next step.

COMPREHENSIVE GUIDE LIBRARY
Deep-dive guides cover everything from COBRA insurance elections to probate filing, from custody arrangements to SSDI applications. Each guide includes step-by-step instructions, word-for-word phone scripts, key terms explained simply, and common mistakes to avoid.

AI NAVIGATOR
Have a question at 2am? Your AI Navigator is available anytime to answer questions, explain next steps, or help you understand complex paperwork, all in warm, judgment-free language.

DEADLINE TRACKING
Never miss a critical deadline. Lumeway tracks time-sensitive items like insurance elections, legal filings, and benefit applications, and reminds you before they are due.

DOCUMENT MANAGEMENT
Keep track of which documents you need, which you have gathered, and upload copies of important paperwork right from your phone.

7 TRANSITIONS COVERED
- Job Loss and Income Crisis
- Death and Estate Settlement
- Divorce and Separation
- Disability and Benefits
- Moving and Relocation
- Retirement Planning
- Addiction and Recovery

BUILT FOR REAL PEOPLE IN REAL CRISES
Lumeway was designed for people in the middle of it. The interface is calm. The language is clear. There are no exclamation points, no toxic positivity, just practical guidance from someone who understands.

Your data is private. Your journey is yours.
