Navigating life transitions is hard enough without having to wonder whether your tools are trustworthy. Here are answers to the questions we hear most.

About Lumeway

Lumeway is a transition guide that helps you navigate major life changes — like job loss, divorce, disability, retirement, relocation, or the death of a loved one. It includes Lumeway Chat for conversational guidance, a personal dashboard to track your progress, step-by-step transition guides, secure document storage, and organizational templates. Everything you need in one place so nothing falls through the cracks.
Lumeway Chat is a conversational guide that walks you through your transition step by step. Describe your situation in your own words and it organizes your next steps, identifies deadlines, suggests what documents you need, and tells you when a professional is needed. It works only with what you share in the conversation — it does not search the internet or access your personal accounts.
The Lumeway dashboard is your personal command center. It includes a checklist tailored to your transition, a calendar for tracking deadlines, secure document storage for important files, step-by-step transition guides, notes, an activity log, and your full chat history — all in one place. Create a free account to start using it.
Lumeway covers six major life transitions: job loss and income crisis, death and estate settlement, divorce and separation, disability and benefits, moving and relocation, and retirement planning.
