Common questions

What's the difference between Starter and the Full Plan? ▾
Starter gives you a template bundle — worksheets you can download and fill out. The Full Plan adds the complete dashboard: step-by-step guides with key terms and contacts, deadline calendar, state-specific guidance, document storage, and more. Your $16 counts toward the upgrade, so you'd only pay $23 more.
What exactly is in the content guides? ▾
Each guide covers a specific task you need to handle — like evaluating health insurance options after a job loss, or navigating probate. For every task, you get: a plain-language explanation of what it is and why it matters, every step in order, key terms defined, who to contact (agencies, phone numbers, websites), common mistakes to avoid, and state-specific rules. It is like having someone walk you through each thing instead of figuring it out alone.
Do my purchases count as credit toward upgrades? ▾
Yes. Every dollar you spend on Lumeway counts toward higher tiers. If you buy a $16 Starter bundle and later want the Full Plan ($39), you only pay the $23 difference. If you've bought one Full Plan ($39) and want Everything ($125), you'd pay $86. Your credit balance shows in your dashboard.
I bought a bundle on Etsy. Does that count? ▾
Yes. Your Etsy bundle came with a redemption code. Enter it in your dashboard to connect your purchase and get $16 in credit toward the Full Plan.
What if I need help with more than one life change? ▾
You can add more life changes for $20 each, or get everything for $125 (minus whatever you've already spent). A lot of people start with one and add more as their situation evolves.
What if I am in financial hardship? ▾
We understand. The free tier provides meaningful support — unlimited conversations with the Navigator, basic checklists, and content previews. If you need more, email hello@lumeway.co and we will work something out.
Do plans expire? ▾
No. Every purchase is one-time and permanent. There are no subscriptions, no renewals, and no surprises. What you buy is yours.
What if it's not what I need? ▾
If your purchase isn't helpful, email hello@lumeway.co within 14 days and we will make it right. We want this to work for you, not add to your stress.
