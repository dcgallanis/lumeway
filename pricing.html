Common questions

What happens after my 30-day Starter trial ends? ▾
Your dashboard reverts to the free tier. You keep your template bundle forever. Your checklist and notes are saved. You can get a Pass anytime to get full access back.
Can I switch my Pass to a different life change? ▾
Not automatically, but reach out to hello@lumeway.co and we will take care of it. If you need access to more than one, the Unlimited plan is usually the better fit.
What if I am in financial hardship? ▾
We understand. Many of our users are navigating financial stress at the same time. The free tier provides meaningful support. If you need more, email hello@lumeway.co and we will work something out.
Can I cancel the Unlimited subscription anytime? ▾
Yes. Cancel anytime from your dashboard settings or email us. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period.
Can someone else buy me a plan as a gift? ▾
Gift passes are coming soon. For now, email hello@lumeway.co and we can set up a gift purchase for you.
