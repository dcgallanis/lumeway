Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

Lumeway is an early-stage product. This policy reflects our current practices and will be updated as the product grows. We are committed to being transparent about how we handle your information.

Who we are

Lumeway ("we," "us," or "our") is an AI-powered Transition Navigator available at lumeway.co. We help individuals navigate major life changes by providing planning tools, process guidance, and support.

Information we collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Waitlist signups: If you join our waitlist, we collect your name and email address via Tally.
  • Chat conversations: When you use the Lumeway chat, your messages are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate responses. We do not permanently store your conversation history on our servers.
  • Usage data: We may collect basic analytics such as page views to understand how people use our site.

How we use your information

  • To send you waitlist updates and product announcements (if you signed up)
  • To provide and improve the Lumeway chat experience
  • To understand how our product is being used

Third-party services

Lumeway uses the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic Claude API: Your chat messages are processed by Anthropic. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Tally: Used for waitlist signups. See Tally's Privacy Policy.
  • Railway: Used to host our application. See Railway's Privacy Policy.

Sensitive information

We understand that conversations about life transitions may involve sensitive personal circumstances. We treat all information shared with Lumeway with the utmost discretion. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data retention

Waitlist email addresses are retained until you unsubscribe. Chat conversations are not permanently stored by Lumeway — each session starts fresh.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your email address from our waitlist at any time
  • Ask what information we hold about you
  • Opt out of any communications from us

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@lumeway.co.

Cookies

Lumeway does not currently use tracking cookies. Basic session data may be stored in your browser to maintain your chat session.

Children's privacy

Lumeway is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as our product evolves. We will post any changes on this page with an updated date.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@lumeway.co.

Lumeway is an AI guide, not a licensed professional. Always consult a qualified advisor.
