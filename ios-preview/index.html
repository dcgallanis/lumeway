What should I do first with the life insurance claim?
Start by gathering the death certificate (you'll need an original certified copy, not a photocopy). Then contact the insurance company's claims department directly. Most companies have a dedicated bereavement line.

You'll need the policy number, the deceased's Social Security number, and your ID as beneficiary. The insurer will send you claim forms. Most claims are processed within 30-60 days once paperwork is complete.
